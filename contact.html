Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to make a reservation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner and weekends, to ensure you get a table at your preferred time.

What are your operating hours?

We're open 24/7! Our kitchen is always open, and we serve food around the clock. Delivery is available from 6 AM to 11 PM.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We offer delivery within Nairobi from 6 AM to 11 PM. Call us at +254 718 230 650 to place your order.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please mention any restrictions when making your reservation.
