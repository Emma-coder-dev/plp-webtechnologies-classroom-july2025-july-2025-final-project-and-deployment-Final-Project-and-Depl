Our Story

Welcome to Savanna Plate, where we bring the authentic flavors of Kenya to the heart of Nairobi. Our restaurant was born from a passion to share the rich culinary heritage of East Africa with both locals and visitors alike.

Founded in 2018, Savanna Plate has become a beloved gathering place for those seeking genuine Kenyan cuisine. We source our ingredients from local farmers and markets, ensuring that every dish reflects the true taste of our homeland.

From the smoky aroma of Nyama Choma to the comforting warmth of Ugali with Sukuma Wiki, our menu celebrates the diversity and richness of Kenyan cooking traditions. Each recipe has been carefully preserved and passed down through generations, now shared with you in our welcoming space.
